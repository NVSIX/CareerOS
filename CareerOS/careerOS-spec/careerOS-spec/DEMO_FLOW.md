# DEMO FLOW — The Judge's Click Path

This is the sequence a Stage 1 reviewer follows. Every navigation link in this
path MUST work. When build trade-offs arise, protect this path first — a
broken link here costs more than a missing flourish anywhere else.

The full path takes under three minutes. Each step lists what the judge does,
what they see, and the aha moment the screen must land.

---

STEP 1 — Land on `/`
Sees: the headline thesis, three-audience framing, the evidence legend.
Aha: "tracks what happens after" — they understand the differentiator before
clicking anything.
Action: clicks "See how it works".

STEP 2 — `/onboarding`
Does: clicks "Upload resume" → watches parsed claims appear → clicks through
both probe reply chips → reaches completion state.
Aha: the right panel reacting per answer — the system tests claims rather
than collecting them. The "artifact requested" badge shows the evidence model
working at intake.
Action: clicks "View your living portfolio".

STEP 3 — `/candidate/profile`
Sees: Amira's evidence profile — verified / evidence / asserted dots in order,
the trajectory chart with projection, the coaching observation.
Aha: hollow vs solid dots — instantly legible claim verification. Second aha:
the coaching copy cites a cohort and a month number, not generic advice.
Action: navigates to Jobs (nav link).

STEP 4 — `/jobs`
Sees: five listings with mono match scores.
Aha: match scores promise an explanation (and the next screen delivers it).
Action: clicks the Senior Logistics Coordinator row.

STEP 5 — `/jobs/job-001`
Sees: performance fingerprint left, match analysis right — 3 of 4 signals,
the ✗ gap explained with the month-24 cohort line.
Aha: an HONEST match — the system shows the gap and frames it as development,
not rejection. Clicks "Apply to this role", sees the confirmation caption
("…your evidence profile, not just your resume").
Action: flips RoleSwitcher to "Employer view".

STEP 6 — `/employer/dashboard`
Sees: the pipeline table — trajectory match AND onboarding risk side by side;
the "Connect Salesforce" card waiting in the right panel.
Aha: pre-hire and post-hire intelligence in one table. Notes Amira's row.
Action: clicks Amira's row.

STEP 7 — `/employer/candidate/cand-001`
Sees: the SAME evidence profile the candidate sees (consistency = trust),
plus the employer-lens reasoning: gap framed against their own past hires
("60% of your previous successful hires…"), onboarding outlook chart.
Aha: natural-language reasoning with a data basis — explainability the brief
explicitly asks for. This is the core value screen.
Action: back to dashboard → clicks "Connect Salesforce".

STEP 8 — `/employer/salesforce`
Does: reads the Tier-1/Tier-2 boundary diagram → clicks Connect → watches the
three-step connection → sees the first record arrive.
Aha: "Onboarding risk score updated: 72 → 61." The post-hire loop closes on
screen. Privacy boundary answered before anyone asks.
End state: judge has seen all three pillars — evidence model, trajectory
matching, post-hire engine — in one continuous path.

---

## Narrative spine (for the concept brief and walkthrough notes)
1. Claims become evidence (onboarding, profile)
2. Evidence becomes explainable matching (jobs, job detail, employer view)
3. Outcomes feed back and sharpen everything (Salesforce, 72 → 61)

If a reviewer only remembers one thing, it should be: "the platform that
keeps learning after the hire."
